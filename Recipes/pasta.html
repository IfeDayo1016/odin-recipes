Pasta

Description

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat a pot of saltwater big enough to accommodate your cooked pasta plus an inch or 2 of pasta water
  2. When it's boiling, add your pasta
  3. When the pasta boils, drain and rinse the pasta in cool water to stop the cooking process
  4. Add a sauce which you have prepared or simply add garlic and oil
